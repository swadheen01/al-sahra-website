About Us

A Trusted Technical Manpower
Partner in the UAE

NABAA AL SAHRA TECHNICAL WORKS LLC is a Dubai-based company specializing in the supply of skilled and semi-skilled technical manpower to businesses across the UAE.

We serve construction companies, main contractors, sub-contractors, and interior fit-out companies — providing reliable, experienced workers ready to integrate into project teams from day one. Our licensed activities include carpentry and wood flooring works, plaster works, and steel products installation and maintenance.

Every worker we deploy is vetted, experienced, and committed to maintaining the safety and quality standards your project demands. We understand that your project timeline is non-negotiable — which is why we operate with professional management and rapid deployment capability.

  • Professional management team
  • Rapid & reliable deployment
  • Committed to safety standards
